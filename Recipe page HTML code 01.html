    Spicy Garlic Udon Noodles

    These spicy garlic udon noodles are chewy and savory with a garlicky spicy
    sauce, and a winner for a quick weekday meal. If you want to jazz them up even
    more, serve topped with a fried egg or stir in some sautéed shrimp

    Nutrition Facts

    Calories: 233 | Fat: 7g | Protein: 9g | carbs: 95g

    Ingredients

    Sauce

    • 4 garlic cloves, finely minced

    • 2 tablespoons soy sauce

    • 2 tablespoons hoisin sauce

    • 2 teaspoons sesame oil

    • 1/2 teaspoon crushed red pepper flakes, or to taste

    Noodles

    • 2 (8 ounce) blocks frozen udon noodles (such as Sanuki®

    • 1 tablespoon sliced green onions

    • 1 teaspoon toasted sesame seeds

    Directions

      Step 1

    • Prepare the sauce by combining garlic, soy sauce, hoisin, sesame oil, and red
      pepper flakes in a small bowl; set aside.

      • Step 2

    • Bring a large pot of water to a boil, and add udon blocks. Let them sit in the
      water until they start to unwind, 1 to 2 minutes. Noodles should remain a little
      chewy. Drain noodles, and return to the pot.

      • Step 3

    • Add sauce, and stir until noodles are well coated. Move to a serving dish, garnish
      with sliced green onions and sesame seeds, and serve.
